Use the following console commands to rename certain Russian cities, regardless of who owns them.

event germany.999 - renames Stalingrad to Hindenburg and Leningrad to Ludendorff
event hgo.993 - renames Stalingrad back to Stalingrad
event hgo.994 - renames Leningrad back to Leningrad
event hgo.995 - renames Stalingrad to Tsaritsyn
event hgo.996 - renames Leningrad to Petrograd
event hgo.997 - renames Stalingrad to Volgograd
event hgo.998 - renames Leningrad to Sankt-Peterburg
event hgo.999 - renames Leningrad to Saint Petersburg
